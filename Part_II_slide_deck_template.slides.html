Investigation Overview¶

  • The majority of the users are males and between 20 - 40 years old.

Dataset Overview¶

The Ford GoBike data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area.This data collected from Feb 2019.

The Gender and age group that are more likely to use GoBike¶

As we can see that the most of GoBike riders are males and their age group are between 20 - 40 years old

Duartion in minute for each day of a week to each user type¶

the chart showed that Saturday and Sunday having the most duration time in minute.The Subscribers were having a less duration time compared to Customers.Which means that Customers are more likely to spends more time in a trip.

The Start Rentals hour and day for Customers¶

the chart show that the Start hour of the weekdays different from weekends days with a high demands in the afternoon and above while in the weekdays the high demands are at 8am and 5pm.

The Start Rentals hour and day for Subscriber¶

As we can see from the chart which is very interesting is that the weekend days is the lowest Number of rentals for the Subscribers which could means that the most of the Subscribers used the GoBike system for their work and also with the same pattern in the rental start hour in the weekdays.
